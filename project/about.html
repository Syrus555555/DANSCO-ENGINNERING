Our Story

Built on Precision.
Trusted for Decades.

Dansco Engineering, LLC has provided quality structural engineering services since 1987. Founded by Samuel A. Greenberg, P.E., the firm has grown from a Florida-based practice into a nationally licensed engineering consultancy serving contractors, developers, and property owners across more than 30 states.

We pride ourselves on delivering accurate, code-compliant engineering in a timely manner. Our clients return to us because we make complex engineering straightforward — and because we pick up the phone.

  • Licensed professional engineers with decades of combined field experience
  • Fast turnaround on stamped drawings and engineering letters
  • Responsive, personalized service on every project — regardless of size
  • Trusted by general contractors, subcontractors, and developers nationwide
